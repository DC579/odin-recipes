Chocolate Chip Caramel Cookies

Description

These chocolate chip caramel cookies have crispy edges and chewy centers. They are loaded with caramel & chocolate chips and topped with flaky sea salt for complete cookie perfection. They're easy to make no chilling required!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup salted butter softened
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 cup caramels (cut into small piece, or caramel chips)
  • ½ cup chocolate chips
  • Flaky sea salt for topping

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line two large baking sheets with silicone baking mats or parchment paper, set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine baking soda, baking powder, and sea salt. Set aside.
  3. In the bowl of a standing mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, or in a large bowl with a hand-held mixer, beat together the butter, white sugar and brown sugar on medium-high speed until light and fluffy about 1 minute.
  4. Add egg and vanilla and beat until combined.
  5. Add the dry ingredients, beat on medium speed until combined.
  6. Add the caramel and chocolate chips and stir until evenly distributed throughout the dough.
  7. Use a 1 ½ TBS cookie scoop to measure out portions of dough, roll them into balls and place them evenly spaced on the two baking sheets (about 8 per sheet). Repeat until all the dough is used.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 8-9 minutes, or until the top is just set and just barely beginning to turn light golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and immediately sprinkle with flaky sea salt. Then, let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5-10 minutes.
  10. After 5-10 minutes, transfer the cookies to a wire rack to cool completely.
